Dr Panos Mavros

I am an architecturally trained spatial cognition scientist living in Singapore. Currently I work as a senior post-doctoral researcher at the Singapore-ETH Centre and the Chair of Cognitive Science, ETH Zrich. I love talking about cities, urban design, navigation, and cycling.

Bio

I hold a PhD on Advanced Spatial Analysis from the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA), The Bartlett, University College London (UCL), studied Digital Media and Culture at the University of Edinburgh and Archicture Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA).
